Objective

Production Solutions offer complete water injection services using specialised technology as an effective way to extend the production life of the well. Enhancing production of hydrocarbon reserves and reducing the environmental impact through re-injection of treated and filtered produced water, this service is proven to be one of the most economical methods for reservoir management.

Sea Water Injection Mobile System

Sea Water Injection Mobile System (SWIMS) is a system employed to increase recovery efficiency through water flooding small marginal offshore oil reservoirs. Specially designed packages are used to filtered and chemically treated seawater to clean up the well reservoir. The system involves the use of portable units which consists of;

  • High Pressure Pump
  • Submersible Pump
  • Chemical Injection Pump
  • Water Holding Tank
  • Gas Compressor
  • Surface Safety Valve

Produced Water Injection Mobile System (PRISM)

Produced Water injection Mobile System (PRISM) is designed to use filtered and chemical treated produced water and re-inject it directly to candidate wells into formation replacing depleting reservoir, displacing oil from the reservoir increasing the “recovery factor”.

This is also used to prevent low pressure in the reservoir, to sweep or displace oil from the reservoir, and push it towards a producing well. The water replaces the oil which has been produced, keeping the production rate and the pressure management over the long term. The system involves the use of portable units which consists of;

  • High Pressure Pump
  • Chemical Injection Pump
  • Water Holding Tank
  • Gas Compressor
  • Surface Safety Valve
