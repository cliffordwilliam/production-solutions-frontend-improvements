About Us

The Group was effectively “born” in Thailand with the creation of Production Solutions HK, a company set up to provide Well Unloading, Well Enhancement inclusive of Sand Clean up and flow back services both to the Thailand and surrounding country Oil and Gas companies.

Business activities commenced in the year of 2012 with the incorporation of Production Solutions, a company initially registered in Hong Kong. A joint venture was formed intent to establish a local Thailand based company structure to provide Well Unloading and Well Enhancement services. It is our aim to use services and materials available from the Thailand Industry sector when available; in circumstances where materials are not readily available we aim to work with local partners to both develop local manufacturing skills and enhance local manufacturing capabilities – this in turns allows us to offer support at a local level whilst providing a quicker delivery timetable in many case.

The principles consist of Addenmay Limited a BVI registered company and Prompt Solutions and Thailand registered company.

Through its JV structure Production Solutions intends to create a local entity in which to undertake all Thailand Well Services operations, and to position itself as a leading provider for Oil and Gas Well Services. Production Solutions plans to promote the use of Thailand based services and labour to the Oil and Gas industry.

Production Solutions is aggressively pursuing the business opportunities in the with a policy of providing a first class service to clients and supporting a service based business built around well testing and production services.

Training Schemes

Production Solutions prioritizes the training & education of our international and national workforce throughout the region. Our in house training matrix has been developed to maximize talents and ensure minimal risk to our people in the work place.

Since launching our service range in the Asia Pacific oil & gas industry we have a dedicated focus is to establish close relations with the leading institutions and continually work towards the further development of local skills & expertise.

Facility Locations

Regional operations are based out of Bangkok with main service centres in Songklha and Sattahip, Thailand. The Production Solutions corporate office is located in Singapore and there are regional operational locations in;

  • Jakarta
  • Indonesia
  • Perth, Adelaide Australia
  • Canada
  • Labuan, Malaysia

Our bases are strategically located to provide efficient equipment maintenance and fast mobilization to both onshore and offshore sectors.

Partners

Productions Solutions has a strategic alliance with Prompt Solutions.

Prompt Solutions is part of the Adisorn Group established to provide required services to petroleum and other related industries.

Prompt Solutions is committed to improving performance through effective management of quality, safety, training and personnel.

They also provide first class technical training services to Thai nationals to develop skills to people of all backgrounds.
