Objective

Sand and solids removal package from either production wells shut in or post fracture, CTU clean up. The Production Solutions package will allow previously high sand producing wells to flow, ensuring no upsets or damage to downstream production equipment and provide significant benefits in terms of Oil & Gas Production.

All Production Solutions filtration units are operated by experienced field personnel trained to the highest standards. All build codes follow intentional industry standards.

  • API SPEC 6A – Pressure Control Equipment
  • API SPEC12J – Oil & Gas Separator
  • API RP 14 E&C – Package Design & Safe Systems
  • API RP 520 – Installation of Pressure Relief device.
  • ANSI B16.5 – Flange class rating
  • ANSI B31.3 – Process Piping
  • NACE MR-01-75 Sour Service
  • LPS / WUU services allow operators to produce additional volumes of oil and gas that were previously unachievable through existing facilities.
  • Flowing Well Head Pressure – 20 ~ 250 psi
  • Oil flow-rate – 500 ~ 6,000 BOPD
  • Gas flow-rate – 1 ~ 3 mmscft/day
  • Water flow-rate – 500 ~ 6,000 BPD
  • Header re-injection pressure – 300 ~ 750 psi
  • Gas compressor suction pressure – 20 ~ 150 psi
  • Gas lift injection pressure – 1,200 psi
  • Gas lift injection volume – 2 mmscft/day
  • WUU preparation will consist of;
  • Platform Visit / PFD and P&ID
  • Equipment Layout with Hazardous classification Cause & Effect Matrix HAZOP / HAZID
  • Load Out Plans
  • Commissioning and Start Up Schedule
