Objective

Depends on the varied nature of the well effluent, there are instances where the produced fluid has to be treated with different chemicals. Our air/gas driven, skid mounted standalone construction with or without chemical tank, high pressure, low volume chemical injection pumps are used for;

  • Prevention of hydrate formation
  • Cure forming problems
  • Reduce H2S content
  • Reduce wax build up
  • Reduce surface tension

Feature & Benefits

  • Fully independent system, can be deployed with local air/gas supply or with one of our optional air compressors.
  • Modular – can quickly and easily install in just hours
  • Ports with valves and flow setting gauge to monitor
  • Optional electric models available upon request
