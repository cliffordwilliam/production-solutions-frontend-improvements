Objective

Sand and solids removal package from either production wells shut in or post fracture, CTU clean up. The Production Solutions package will allow previously high sand producing wells to flow, ensuring no upsets or damage to downstream production equipment and provide significant benefits in terms of Oil & Gas Production.

All Production Solutions filtration units are operated by experienced field personnel trained to the highest standards. All build codes follow intentional industry standards.

We have an increasing fleet size and range of both dual & single pot sand filters. Pressure rating on our sand filtration units range between 5,000 psi & 10,000 psi.

Designed to improve our clients analysis during testing operations, completion clean-ups and maximum sand-free rate tests. The unit packages are skid mounted and fully certified for offshore projects.

Specifically engineered units give us the smallest footprint currently available in the market today.

All build codes follow intentional industry standards:

  • NACE MR 0175
  • API-6A
  • ANSI B31.3

Designed to suit individual client requirements.

  • Well Head 5,000 psi
  • Shut Down Valve 5,000 psi w/ 2” & 3” fig 1502
  • Double Block Valves w/ Equalizing Port
  • Sand Screen Filter 50, 100, 200 & 400 Microns
  • Gas Capacity: 25 MMSCFD
  • Liquid Capacity: 7,000 BPD
  • Working Temp: – 20°c to 121°c
  • Volume: Single 28.19 L / Double 56.38 L
  • Filter Screen: Various Micron sizes
  • Inlet / Outlet: 2” & 3” Fig 1502
  • Footprint: 200 x 120 x 280 cm
