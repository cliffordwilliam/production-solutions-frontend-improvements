Objective

Test wells to determine its flow capacity at specific conditions of reservoir and flowing pressures. Flow potential, annular pressure, critical flow rate, net gas production and water/oil ratio. We can offer mini well test separator for spot check of existing wells or larger package for well clean up after drilling campaign (Rigless).

Production Solutions offer Clean-up packages which includes surface production equipment and pre-production, high rate gas flow rate measurement, water handling and treatment & solids handling systems.

Well Testing is used to determine the flow capacity at specific conditions of reservoir and flowing pressures. Flow potential, annular pressure, critical flow rate, net gas production and water/oil ratio are determined.
